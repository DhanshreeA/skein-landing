The toolkit

Everything is an experiment. Skein keeps the receipts.

Treat every shop change like a run — version it, measure it, and let the numbers settle the debate.

Experiment runs

Spin up a run for any listing change — photo, title, price, bundle. Each run captures a clean before/after so a hunch becomes a measurable test.

Variant compare

Line up A, B, and baseline side by side. Skein runs the significance math so you ship the winner with confidence, not a coin flip.

SEO tag lab

Test all 13 tags as variants and watch which keywords pull search traffic. Tag swaps become tracked runs, not guesswork in a spreadsheet.

Revenue attribution

Tie every dollar back to the change that earned it. See revenue per listing per variant, so you know exactly which tweak paid for itself.

Auto-insights

Skein watches your runs and surfaces the takeaways in plain language: “Lifestyle photos lifted conversion 54% on home goods.” No charts to decode.

Shop leaderboard

Rank every listing by the metric you care about and benchmark against anonymized shops in your craft category. Know where you actually stand.

How it works

From hunch to proven win in three steps.

01

Connect your shop

Link your Etsy shop in a minute. Skein backfills your listing history and metrics, so you start with a real baseline — not a blank slate.

02

Launch an experiment

Pick a listing and a thing to test — photo, title, price, tags. Skein versions both variants and starts logging views, favorites, and conversion automatically.

03

Ship the winner

When a variant wins with confidence, promote it in one click and roll the learning into your next run. Compounding gains, fully documented.
